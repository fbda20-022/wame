All About Johannesburg

Jo'burg as popularly knows is the largest city in South Africa and clasified as a megacity. It is the provincial capital and largest city of Gauteng,which is the wealthiest province in South Africa.

Location

The city is located in the north-eastern part of South Africa in the mineral-rich Witwatersrand range of hills which is the centre of large-scale gold and diamond trade.It is bordered by the provinces of Limpopo on the north, Mpumalanga on the east, Free State on the south, and North West on the west.

History of the City

The city was established in 1886 following the discovery of gold on what had been a farm. Due to the extremely large gold deposit found along the Witwatersrand, within ten years, the population had grown to 100,000 inhabitants.By 1924 as the scale of gold deposits became apparent, Johannesburg transformed from a dusty farm into a beautiful city with skyscrapers.
